🧁 Baking tips

Small habits, better bakes

01

Weigh, don't scoop

A kitchen scale gives far more consistent results than measuring cups, especially for flour.

02

Room temp ingredients

Butter and eggs blend more evenly when they're not cold, leading to a better rise.

03

Don't skip the rest

Letting dough rest lets the gluten relax, making a noticeable difference in texture.

04

Preheat fully

Give your oven the full preheat time — opening the door early lets heat escape.

👗 Style tips

Small habits, longer-lasting clothes

01

Wash jeans less often

Frequent washing breaks down denim fibers faster — spot clean when you can instead.

02

Cold water, always

Hot water shrinks fabric and fades color much faster than a cold cycle.

03

Air dry knitwear

Tumble drying breaks down knit fibers — lay flat to dry instead to preserve shape.

04

Use the right hangers

Wooden or padded hangers keep shoulders from stretching out of shape over time.
